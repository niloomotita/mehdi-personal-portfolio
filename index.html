  • Mehdi Rahimi

    Born in 24 February 1983-Iran
    Visual Arts – BA, Mazandaran University, Iran

  • Anargol

    I've been working as a graphic designer and marketing manager of Anargol since 2012, taking care of all the the marketing activities of Anargol at Divar Studio

    This has been a great experience for me, because as a designer I must take a larger audience in consideration and as a photographer I should come up with a new style for each collection. As a result my experience with Anargol has been more like an installation to me.
